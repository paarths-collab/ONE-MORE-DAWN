# One More Dawn - 50-Second Demo Script

**Hard cap:** final export <= 49 seconds.
**Format:** 1080p landscape gameplay capture, real UI, short captions, and
voice-over.
**Core message:** *A community does not receive a city. It builds one.*

This version is deliberately honest about the live V1: use the real city,
decisions, Dawn Report, and aftermath. Do not invent shield-point counters,
casualty numbers, wall destruction, or a playable expedition that the live game
does not show.

## Shot list

### 0:00-0:06 - The Camp

Show the empty Camp at dawn: few structures, little protection, and the city
vitals.

**Caption:** `EVERY SUBREDDIT STARTS WITH ONE CAMP.`
**Voice-over:** "One More Dawn is a community survival game on Reddit. Every
subreddit begins with one shared city."

### 0:06-0:13 - A place in the city

Show role selection, choose one of the six roles, take one real daily action, and
show the first-house feedback or a contributor house in the city.

**Caption:** `ONE REDDITOR. ONE HOUSE.`
**Voice-over:** "Choose a survivor role, spend your daily energy, and your first
accepted contribution gives you a place in the city."

### 0:13-0:20 - Growth is shared

Show shared labor, the next civic building, contributor homes, and a city-growth
transition or seeded mid-run city.

**Caption:** `BUILT TOGETHER, DAWN BY DAWN.`
**Voice-over:** "No one owns the city alone. Community labor turns a Camp into a
settlement worth protecting."

### 0:20-0:29 - Decide together

Show the LIVE panel: one crisis vote, one Council strategy, The Marked pledge,
and a brief City Chatter view.

**Caption:** `VOTE. PLEDGE. DECIDE.`
**Voice-over:** "The city faces a crisis each day. The community votes, backs a
strategy, and decides what - or who - it refuses to lose."

### 0:29-0:36 - Restore a district

Show Reconnect the City, rotate a few tiles, then show the restored connection.

**Caption:** `RECONNECT THE CITY.`
**Voice-over:** "A daily district puzzle gives every survivor another way to
restore what the city needs."

### 0:36-0:44 - Dawn has consequences

Show Raid Watch, then use force-resolve in a controlled demo. Show the actual
dome result, Dawn Report, and any visible aftermath or reconstruction target.

**Caption:** `DAWN REMEMBERS.`
**Voice-over:** "At dawn, the city answers for its preparation. The dome holds,
or the community carries what the night damaged."

### 0:44-0:49 - Rebuild and return

Show reconstruction, a sunrise city view, then the One More Dawn mark and the
real public play link.

**Caption:** `BUILD TOGETHER. SURVIVE TOGETHER.`
**Voice-over:** "When a home falls, the city rebuilds it together. Can your
community survive one more dawn?"

## Recording gates

- Use a real private or public Devvit post for all player-facing UI.
- Seed demo state and force-resolve are moderator tools for controlled recording;
  identify the footage as a seeded demo if that context matters.
- Do not record demo-only cinematic raid effects as proof of live V1 gameplay.
- Replace the end-card placeholder with the real Reddit post URL before export.
- Keep the finished file under 49 seconds and export at 1080p landscape.
